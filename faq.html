#FAQ(?)? or Questions you might ask yourself:

Q1 : Who would be the agents?

Students or people from all walks of life who decide on their availability. All will have to go through a selection process (examination, record check and criminal record, references, etc.)

Q3 : Why not use the services of USPS and Canada Post competitors?

The costs are less attractive and their facilities (points of service too far away, fewer points of service, etc.) do not achieve our environmental and economic objectives. There are fewer points of service and especially in remote areas.
